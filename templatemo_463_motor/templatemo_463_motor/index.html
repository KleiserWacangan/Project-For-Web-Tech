About the site

Our website focuses on providing random individuals with the intent to learn about fun facts regarding historical events, trivia and modern information. Regardless of age, status, religion, gender or sexual orientation, our website offers a non-biased information people are looking for. Our program makes sure that users are always updated on new discoveries and information that they would surely be most interested in.
